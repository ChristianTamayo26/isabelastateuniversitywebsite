ISABELA STATE UNIVERSITY

About ISU Cauayan Campus

Welcome to Isabela State University Cauayan Campus

Isabela State University (ISU) is a premier educational institution in the Philippines committed to providing quality education for sustainable development. The Cauayan Campus is one of the strategic campuses of ISU, dedicated to serving the educational needs of the Isabela community and beyond.

Campus Overview

Located in Cauayan, Isabela, our campus is equipped with modern facilities and staffed by dedicated educators committed to academic excellence. The campus serves thousands of students across various undergraduate and graduate programs.

History

Established as part of ISU's expansion initiative, the Cauayan Campus has grown to become a center of academic excellence, offering diverse programs in agriculture, engineering, education, business, and information technology.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Commitment to high academic standards
  • Innovation: Integration of modern teaching methodologies
  • Sustainability: Focus on environmental and social responsibility
  • Inclusivity: Creating opportunities for all students
  • Community Service: Contributing to societal development

Academic Leadership

The campus is administered by qualified administrators and academic officers dedicated to providing supportive learning environments and fostering student success.
